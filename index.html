LLVG: A Detective Adventure for GRE Prep
A game adaption of a famous detective book.
Note:
To play the chapters, you need to earn enough scores by playing mini-game1/2.
Each chapter has its requirement of scores.
In the FREE version, all buttons are unlocked after you enter mini-game1 once. You can explore freely without the requirements of scores.
In the FORMAL version, you have to earn at least 5/7/10 scores to unlock the subsequent function.
